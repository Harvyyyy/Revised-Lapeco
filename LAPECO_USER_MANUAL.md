AUTHENTICATION   	
Figure 1: Login Page
1.    Login Field – Enter your email address or username in this field. This is your unique identifier in the system.
2.    Password Field – Enter your password. Ensure you use a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters for improved security.
3.    Show Password Toggle – Click the eye icon to reveal or hide your password while typing.
4.    Remember Me Checkbox – Tick this option if you want the system to keep you signed in on your device for future visits.
5.    Forgot Password Link – Click this if you forgot your password. Enter your email in the popup to initiate the reset. A password reset link will be sent to your inbox. You must open that link from the email to access the password reset screen.
6.    Login Button – Click this to log in and access the system. Ensure that your credentials are correct before clicking.
 
7.    Account Lockout Protection – If you enter incorrect credentials multiple times, your account will be temporarily locked for security purposes. A countdown timer will display the remaining lockout time.
 
Figure 2: Forgot Password
1.    Email Address Field – Enter the email address associated with your LAPECO account.
2.    Send Reset Code Button – Click this to send a password reset code to your email address.
3.    Verification Code Field – Enter the 6-digit code sent to your email.
4.    New Password Field – Create a new secure password using letters, numbers, and special characters.
5.    Confirm Password Field – Re-enter your new password to ensure it matches.
6.    Reset Password Button – Click this to confirm and reset your password.
7.    Back to Login Link – Click to return to the login page.
 
Figure 3: Force Password Change
1.    Current Password Field – Enter your existing password for verification.
2.    New Password Field – Create a new secure password with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
3.    Confirm Password Field – Re-enter your new password to confirm.
4.    Password Requirements – Review the password strength requirements displayed on the page.
5.    Change Password Button – Click this to update your password and proceed to the dashboard.
